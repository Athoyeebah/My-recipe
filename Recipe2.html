Baked salmon

What sets this recipe apart is it's simplicity.With only a few ingredients and minimal prep time, you can create a restaurant-quality dish in the comfort of your own home.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, parsley, dill, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Brush the lemon-herb mixture evenly over both sides of the salmon fillets.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until cooked through, depending on thickness.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with additional parsley and lemon wedges if desired.
