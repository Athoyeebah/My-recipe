Grilled Chicken Fajitas

This classic dish is a staple for a reason -it's easy, delicious, and perfect for a quick weeknight dinner for a weekend gathering

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast.
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together lime juice, garlic, oregano, and cumin. Add chicken and marinate for 30 minutes
  2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade, letting excess liquid drip off.
  3. Grill chicken for 5-6 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Transfer to a plate and cover with foil.
  4. Grill onion and bell peppers for 5-7 minutes per side, or until tender.
  5. Warm tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  6. Assemble fajitas by slicing chicken and vegetables, and serving with tortillas, salt, pepper, and desired toppings
